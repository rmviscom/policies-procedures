# Team Meeting Schedule

## Weekly Team Meetings

The Marketing and Communications team participates in a weekly [Teams](https://help.buildfore.com/policies-procedures-marcomm/marketing-and-communications-team-101/software-and-subscriptions/microsoft-teams) meeting Tuesday mornings at 9:30am Central/10:30am Eastern.

The second Tuesday of each month, our team spends time focused around our AI usage and best practices for our AI tool [Joist](https://help.buildfore.com/policies-procedures-marcomm/marketing-and-communications-team-101/software-and-subscriptions/joist-ai).

### **Invitation**

Please contact the meeting organizer, [Adria Klein](mailto:aklein@robinsmorton.com), to be added to the meeting invitation.

### **Meeting Agenda**

The agenda for the meetings comes from our Monday.com General Marketing Dashboard and [can be found here](https://robins-unit.monday.com/overviews/3561782).

### **Recordings**

Tuesday meetings are recorded and posted in Teams in the [Marketing Meetings and Workshops](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A3951a22bef07477f8ba9a008402f0d12%40thread.skype/Marketing%20Meetings%20and%20Workshops?groupId=bbf0c79c-0873-4a7d-a74a-d0a94914ba90\&tenantId=c23722fc-bdea-442c-bd58-748d8281bc73) channel.

This meeting is an opportunity to share information with or request information from the team at large. Typical agenda items are:

* Anyone who needs help
* 5-minute shares
* Longer shares from inside or outside the department These are scheduled in advance and found on the [monday.com General Marketing Dashboard](https://robinsmorton-global.monday.com/overviews/16533180). Contact [Katy Klaproth](mailto:kklaproth@robinsmorton.com) to schedule a share.
* Upcoming deadlines and events
* Design/Branding/Ranking updates company-wide
* Unanet.com opportunity updates
* Monday.com sales pursuit updates
* Small Wins posted in the corresponding channel on Teams
* Additional topics, as needed

## Marketing Workshops

The Marketing and Communications team participates in departmental two-day workshops. The workshops occur both virtually and in-person depending on the workload and travel flexibility of the team.

Workshop planning begins 4-6 weeks in advance. Dates and location are decided based on the needs and availability for the majority of the team

In the workshops we review the following items:&#x20;

* Annual strategic goals
  * Marketing/Sales Support
  * Asset Management
  * Branding
* Communications
* Review Vision Statement
* Review Policies & Procedures
* Review Unanet Knowledge updates
* Study Action Teams

In addition to the meeting, we will do a team building activity as well as a jobsite visit to a local project.&#x20;
