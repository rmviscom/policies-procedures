# Department Team Cheat Sheet

## Marketing Team

* **Sales pursuits**
  * Manage RFP and presentation process
* **Project marketing**&#x20;
  * Project events
  * Sharing your project story
  * Scheduling project photography
* **Office marketing support**
  * Office event support (i.e., open houses)
  * Scheduling corporate photo shoots when needed
* **Branding and communications**
  * Maintain consistency and be aware of standards
  * Provide artwork for ads or sponsorships upon request
  * Request comms and graphics support as needed
* **Award submissions as needed**
* **Participate in regular site visits**
* **Reporting**
  * Maintain Unanet records and team member resumes
  * Board report - coordinate aerial photography when requested
  * Maintain/update corporate events calendar

## Communications team

* **External communication**
  * Handle media inquiries
  * Support speaking opportunities
  * Publish State of Healthcare Construction annually
  * Publish and maintain brochures and sales collateral
  * Publish, manage, and maintain robinsmorton.com
* **Crisis communication**
  * Liaise with contracted PR firm when needed
* **Awards**
  * Manage awards process
  * Support regional marketers with awards entries
* **Training**
  * Provide media training to teams as requested
* **Surveys**
  * Manage and complete survey responses
* **Internal communication**
  * Publish Newscorner quarterly
  * Publish Framework weekly
  * Publish and maintain In-house Style Guide, messaging and branding maps
  * Publish other corporate documents and reports as requested
  * Support internal communications campaigns
* **Sales support**
  * Collaborate on strategic sales pursuits as needed

### HUB team

* **Oversee HUB content**
* **Train users**
* **Support team members in creating their own content**
* **Liaise with software vendor**



## Branding and Graphics team

* **Publish and maintain brand standards**
* **Project support**
  * Provide custom art for project events and promo items as needed
  * Design and order promotional items upon request
* **Sales support**
  * Provide custom graphics/templates for sales pursuits as needed
  * Create and maintain RFP templates
  * Create and maintain presentation templates
  * Provide support for opportunities as needed
* **Internal support**
  * Design corporate documents and reports
  * Design Newscorner
  * Provide graphics for internal communications campaigns
  * Create and maintain design and document templates for all team members
  * Manage printing for corporate documents
  * Provide slides for internal meetings and events
  * Provide custom art for internal teams as requested
* **External support**
  * Provide social graphics as needed
  * Provide graphics for external speaking opportunities
  * Provide graphics for external documents
  * Create and maintain ad templates
* **Promotional item support**
  * Oversee the company store - manage stock and create new products/designs
  * Liaise with promotional item vendors to ensure quality and consistency
  * Custom art - provide designs for ERS and project events as needed
* **Training**
  * Provide support and training as needed for InDesign and other software or processes

## Holiday team

* **Corporate holiday campaign**
  * Creative concept
  * Employee gifts
  * Client gifts
  * Holiday card
  * Landing page
  * Submits award entry



## Asset Management team

* Assists in enters and maintains Unanet data
* Experts on Unanet and OpenAsset
* Assists with pulling resumes and project profiles for RFPs

### Photography team

* Schedules and manages photo shoots for projects, corporate campaigns, and headshots
* Collaborates with outside photographers
* Manages photo storage via OpenAsset
