# Microsoft Teams

## Video Conferences

Teams is the preferred video conference platform for internal and external Robins & Morton communications. All salaried team members have access to a Microsoft Teams account, and most conference rooms in our corporate and regional offices are equipped to host Teams meetings. Should you need to schedule video conference or hybrid meeting internal to Robins & Morton, use Teams.&#x20;

**If you are scheduling a video conference or hybrid meeting with team members outside of Robins & Morton, you have the option to use Teams or Zoom.** However, if participating members are comfortable with and have access to Teams, it is easiest to host all meetings through Teams.&#x20;

Should you need the Robins & Morton Zoom account, please contact Division Marketing Director [Farrah Mote](mailto:fmote@robinsmorton.com).

IMPORTANT NOTES

Teams is an excellent tool for collaborating with colleagues. However, there are some things to keep in mind:

* **Teams does not allow you to delete messages.** Teams should be used professionally and appropriately.
* **Files and documents should not be saved in Teams.** Please save all documents in the designated Egnyte file structure. For questions, please contact [Adria Klein](mailto:aklein@robinsmorton.com).
* **Teams is an internal resource for Robins & Morton.** Individuals outside of our organization do not have access to Teams.
