# Staffbase

In 2024, we migrated our intranet from [Sharepoint](https://robinsmorton.sharepoint.com/Pages/Home.aspx) to [Staffbase](https://staffbase.com/), a dedicated internal communications platform.

Staffbase allows us to publish internal communication like news and knowledge. It also includes an email module that we use to publish The Framework, as well as other internal emails like new hire welcome communications.&#x20;

Contact [Ginny Fowler](mailto:ginny.fowler@robinsmorton.com) for questions or more info.
