# Adobe

The Marketing and Communications Department has access to the entire Adobe Creative Cloud Suite including InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere, Spark, Acrobat, and more. All design documents should be created using an Adobe application.

Upon joining the Marketing and Communications Department, your supervisor should provide you with an account to the Adobe Creative Cloud. If you need access to Adobe Creative Cloud, please contact [Katy Klaproth](mailto:kklaproth@robinsmorton.com) or [Farrah Mote](mailto:fmote@robinsmorton.com).

Part of the Adobe Creative Cloud includes shared CC Libraries that house Robins & Mortonâ€™s Brand Standards (colors, logos, icons, etc.) and other commonly used items. This ensures all materials are within corporate brand standards. If you do not have access to the CC Libraries, please contact [Katy Klaproth](mailto:kklaproth@robinsmorton.com) or [Farrah Mote](mailto:fmote@robinsmorton.com).

## InDesign OpenAsset Plugin

Robins & Morton houses all photos in OpenAsset, a cloud-based document management tool. For Adobe applications, there is an OpenAsset plugin which allows marketers to easily drag and drop photos from OpenAsset into a live Adobe document.
