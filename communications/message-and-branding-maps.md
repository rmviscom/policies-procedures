# Message and Branding Maps

## Message Map

The message map defines:

* Who we are
* What we do
* How we do it
* Value proposition (why choose Robins & Morton)
* Our values

Utilize the message map to verify all key messaging is consistent with Robins & Morton’s approved corporate messaging.

## Branding Map

The branding map defines each of Robins & Morton’s key brand elements, corresponding strategic uses, target audience, and connection to our primary brand: Building with Purpose®. Our brand elements are:

* &#x20;Building with Purpose® & Building Forward®
* True Builder®
* &#x20;SmartFab®
* &#x20;Giving with Purpose
* &#x20;JET Jobsite Engagement Team
* Pursuit of Safety Excellence®
* Training for Excellence

Utilize the brand map to verify all key messaging is consistent with Robins & Morton’s approved primary and sub brands and messaging.
