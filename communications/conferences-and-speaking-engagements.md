# Conferences and Speaking Engagements

Annually, Robins & Morton team members engage in a variety of conference and speaking opportunities. We encourage everyone to take part in leading our industry with their expertise.&#x20;

View the [Conferences and Speaking Engagements page on HUB](https://hub.robinsmorton.com/content/page/6600ca681d742c3274a510c4) for more detail.

#### Before committing to attend:

* Complete the [Conference Participation Request form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=_CI3wuq9LES9WHSNgoG8cxUw6PsmvOtMtjAg1_F7rLpUMklWNUtKREJBN0c3QTZTWkpGMjhES0Q2MCQlQCN0PWcu).&#x20;

#### Before committing to speak:

* Review the [Conference Speaker Expectations](https://hub.robinsmorton.com/api/media/secure/external/v2/raw/upload/6933108f76fb947a840d1e0d.pdf) document to ensure a successful presentation.
* Complete the [Conference Speaker Preparation Checklist](https://hub.robinsmorton.com/api/media/secure/external/v2/raw/upload/69331089d4eed51b4c5c21f0.pdf).
