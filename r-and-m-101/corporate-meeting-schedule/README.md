# Corporate Meeting Schedule

## Board Meeting

Held quarterly at the corporate office in Birmingham. Attendees include Robins & Morton’s board of directors (invited external community members), Bill Morton, Robin Savage, and Ben Leaver.

Board members receive a printed deliverable (the board report) that provides an overview of the company’s financial status, project updates, safety updates, and personnel reports.

[Learn more about the board report process.](board-report-process.md)



***

## Corporate Meeting

This meeting is held the first Monday of each month to discuss high-level corporate and operational items. A corporate meeting package is prepared and distributed to attendees each month.

The attendees include divisional office leaders, safety and Robin Savage.

Our marketing team provides a summary of all outstanding/in progress sale opportunities that are in the RFQ, RFP, or presentation stage.&#x20;

The corporate meeting package also includes a summary of all regional events from [our marketing calendar on HUB](https://hub.robinsmorton.com/content/page/667db6797bfaae4b458d0594). **Please be sure to keep all event lists up to date so they are accurate for this meeting.**



[Farrah Mote](mailto:fmote@robinsmorton.com) runs this report from Unanet and provides to [Patti Kyker](mailto:pkyker@robinsmorton.com) each month.



***

## Operations Manager (OM) Meeting

This meeting is held every four weeks and allows for detailed discussion on all in-progress projects (schedule, profit, etc.), projects in planning, operational team member project assignments, safety report, self-perform work reports, hiring and recruiting reports, and a training topic.

Attendees include all operations managers, executive leadership, human resources leadership, and safety leadership. Each meeting is led by a different operations manager who is responsible for running the meeting and providing the training topic.

Our Marketing team provides a customer satisfaction summary report of the most recent customer satisfaction survey responses and key client comments. [Katy Klaproth](mailto:kklaproth@robinsmorton.com) manages this report and provides to [Patti Kyker](mailto:pkyker@robinsmorton.com) prior to each month’s meeting.



***

## Sales Forecast Meeting

Sales forecast meetings are corporate-level sessions to review recently won projects.

[Learn more about the Sales Forecast report.](https://robinsmorton.gitbook.io/policies-procedures-marcomms/TG8GDp4XhDH7yo4v3kBh/sales/tracking-and-reporting#sales-forecast-report)



***

## Safety Team Meeting

This meeting is held the second Monday of every month to share key safety statistics and discuss a safety training topic.

This meeting is mandatory for all operational team members. In addition to operational team participation, several other departments also attend including Human Resources, Marketing, and other support departments.

Our team does not provide any content or deliverable for the safety meeting.



***

## Building Forward® Spotlight Call

This meeting is held the fourth Monday of every month. Each meeting has a designated topic and selected team members will share any Building Forward® updates or contributions that speak to the four tenets.

This meeting is open to all Robins & Morton salaried team members to attend.

Our team does not provide any content or deliverable for the Building Forward® Spotlight call, unless we are asked to present.



***

## Partners Meeting

The purpose of this meeting is to discuss company strategy, initiatives, and divisional office updates. Attendees include all company partners.&#x20;

Marketing team members may be asked to help divisional leaders with creating slides for their portion of the meeting. **Divisional leaders are given a PowerPoint template to ensure consistency, so Marketing team members provide content updates only.** Periodically, Marketing team members may be asked to help the designated Partner’s Meeting coordinator (this person changes each year) to help with additional deliverables for the meeting.
