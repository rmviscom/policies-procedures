## Human Resource tools

* **SuccessFactors**\
  A tool used in Robins & Morton’s employee performance review process.\
  [Login to SuccessFactors](https://performancemanager4.successfactors.com/)
* **Guide to the Robins & Morton Way**\
  Employee handbook with information regarding who we are, leave, compensation, learning & development, reporting concerns, reimbursements, and much more.\
  [Guide to the R\&M Way](https://my.robinsmorton.com/app/uploads/2023/09/Salaried-New-Hire-Guide_digital-2025.pdf)
* **Overview of team member benefits**\
  [Benefits Website](https://my.robinsmorton.com/)
* **Sage HR Portal**\
  Central location to perform HR functions including updating personal information, requesting time off, electing benefits, reviewing compensation and pay history, and more.\
  [Sage Login](https://hr.robinsmorton.net/selfservice/)
* **Training for Excellence**\
  Robins & Morton’s internal personnel training initiative featuring live and on-demand training courses. Accessed via Litmos. \
  [Litmos login](https://robinsandmorton.litmos.com)
