# Tradeshows

Marketing participates in tradeshow events by supporting planning, preparation, and delivery.&#x20;

A list of tradeshows the Robins & Morton team attends can be found on the [Event Support page on HUB](https://hub.robinsmorton.com/content/page/65d65bcbfcb0b248a015b09c).

The exact specifications for each of these tradeshow events is typically dictated by the event, depth of involvement and Robins & Morton team members involved.&#x20;

## Giveaways

Tradeshow giveaways can be found on the Company Store at [https://rmcs.conceptsusa.com/promotional.asp](https://rmcs.conceptsusa.com/promotional.asp). If you would like to have a custom event item created for the tradeshow, email [Julianna Danner](mailto:jdanner@conceptsusa.com) or use the Special Requests tab on the company store to start this process. When making a special request, please include:&#x20;

* the type of event, item, or type of item desired
* estimated budget
* quantity
* event date

If you have any questions or need any assistance while sourcing or creating promotional items for events, please contact [Amanda ](mailto:abradley@robinsmorton.com)[Bradley Witteck](mailto:abradley@robinsmorton.com).
