# OP-030 Preconstruction Services Kickoff Meeting - Intracompany

{% hint style="info" %}
## Purpose

To ensure the preconstruction services required by the Client are delivered in a timely and professional manner, by identifying strategies and work assignments.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
## Responsibility

Operations Manager
{% endhint %}

## Policy

To ensure an effective preconstruction service kickoff meeting is executed, the following items should be addressed as a minimum.

1. The Operations Manager shall, at the earliest time after award of a preconstruction service agreement, conduct a preconstruction services kickoff meeting. The Operations Manager, Project Director, Preconstruction Manager/Estimator, Superintendent, and Project Manager assigned to the project should participate.&#x20;
2. The Operations Manager shall use OP-030.1 Preconstruction Services Kickoff Meeting Example Agenda and Form OP-030.2 Preconstruction Estimate Action List as a basis for the meeting agenda.
3. The action list should be utilized as a part of a larger and more comprehensive meeting agenda to develop strategies and manage risk. The Operations Manager should lead the meeting.
4. An initial and appropriate profit-specific risk analysis should be discussed as part of the agenda, to identify items that could impact the success of the project, if not adequately addressed during preconstruction. Items discussed should include, but not be limited to, the following:
   1. Labor availability, source, and skill level
   2. Site conditions
   3. Potential weather impacts due to location
   4. Self-performed work (SPW)
   5. Availability of subcontractors
   6. Schedule issues, deadlines, and phasing
   7. Risk to public
   8. Relationship with the client
   9. Other team members such as architect and engineers
   10. Experience level of the project team
   11. Coordination issues or site use restrictions
   12. Unique design, structure type, long lead items, or constructability issues
   13. Do we have experience with the major design elements?
5. If appropriate for the project, a preconstruction kickoff meeting with the Owner and Architect should be established (Refer to Policy OP-040 Project Delivery Team Kickoff Meeting).
6. Meeting minutes should be produced and distributed within 24 hours of the conclusion of the meeting.
7. Regularly scheduled, formal follow-up meetings should be held throughout the preconstruction process with all Preconstruction, Estimating, and Operations personnel attending (Refer to Policy OP-060 Preconstruction Services Progress Meeting Intracompany).
