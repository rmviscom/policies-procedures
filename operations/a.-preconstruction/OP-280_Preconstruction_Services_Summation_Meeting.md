# OPERATIONS - PRECONSTRUCTION - POLICY OP-280

## TITLE:
Preconstruction Services Summation Meeting - Intracompany

{% hint style="info" %}
**PURPOSE:**

To ensure the Preconstruction Department receives feedback on their efforts from the Project Construction team, by identifying key metrics for use by the Preconstruction department in future projects.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
**RESPONSIBILITY:**

Operations Manager
{% endhint %}

## POLICY:

POLICY:	To ensure an effective preconstruction summation meeting is executed, the following items should be addressed as a minimum.
The Operations Manager shall conduct a Preconstruction Services Summation Meeting. The Operations Manager, Preconstruction Team Members, Superintendent, and Project Manager assigned to the project should participate.  At a minimum, one Preconstruction Services Summation Meeting shall be held after project completion.  Some longer or more complex projects may benefit from additional meetings (e.g. after buy-out, mid-project, etc.).
The Operations Manager may use OP-280.1 Project Summation Meeting – Intracompany – Agenda or OP-280.2 Preconstruction Services Summation Meeting - Intracompany - A3 as an outline for the meeting. The Operations Manager should lead the meeting.
The project’s Subcontractor & Vendor Evaluation Reports should be reviewed from policy OP-270 Subcontractor & Vendor Evaluation Database.
Meeting minutes should be produced and distributed within 24 hours of the conclusion of the meeting.
