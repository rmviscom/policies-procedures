# OS-010 Notice to Proceed

At the appropriate time, after execution of the Contract, the Owner will be requested to send a letter establishing the date work is to commence.

Note: The following must also be complete and on file, prior to starting work:

路 Evidence of financing.

路 Performance and Payment Bonds, if required.

路 Certificate of Insurance (R\&M and all subcontractors on site).

路 Builder's Risk Insurance, if required.
