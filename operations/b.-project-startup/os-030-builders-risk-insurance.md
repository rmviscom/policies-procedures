# OS-030 Builders Risk Insurance



* The Project Manager is responsible for reviewing coverage and requirements with the Operations Manager and Financial Risk Manager.
* [For](https://robinsmorton.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/operations/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B1D6D254A-0911-4EFD-9B90-E4125A955ED2%7D\&file=OS%20140%20BR%20APPLICATION.xlsx\&action=default\&mobileredirect=true)[m ](https://robinsmorton.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/operations/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B1D6D254A-0911-4EFD-9B90-E4125A955ED2%7D\&file=OS%20140%20BR%20APPLICATION.xlsx\&action=default\&mobileredirect=true)[OS-140](https://robinsmorton.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/operations/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B1D6D254A-0911-4EFD-9B90-E4125A955ED2%7D\&file=OS%20140%20BR%20APPLICATION.xlsx\&action=default\&mobileredirect=true) Builders Risk Insurance Information should be provided to the client if the insurance is provided by the client or to the Financial Risk Manager if insurance is to be provided by Robins & Morton.
* If the client provides the insurance, the Project Manager is responsible for obtaining from the client a certificate of insurance reflecting the Builders Risk coverage and naming Robins & Morton and its subcontractors as an additional insured under the policy. It shall also state “Wavier of Subrogation in favor of Robins & Morton”.
* If the client provides the insurance, The Project Manager shall submit the client’s certificate of insurance to the Financial Risk Manager for review and record keeping.
* For unusual circumstances such as jobsites located in hurricane areas, areas known to flood, etc the Lead Estimator shall obtain a quote for the Builders Risk Insurance during preconstruction.
