# OS-021 Contract Agreement

1. Prepare the Contract on the appropriate Form (AIA, DBIA, Client Form, etc.)
2. Any items affecting the risk on the project should be discussed with and approved by the Chief Financial Officer. Special attention should be paid to maximizing the fee for each project.
   1. Fee
   2. Insurance
   3. Liquidated Damages
   4. Early Completion Bonuses
   5. Small Tool Allowance
   6. Reimbursable Items
   7. Savings Split
   8. Self-Performed Work
   9. Other Compensation Clauses
3. After preparation and review by the parties responsible, the contract should be reviewed and initialed by the following before signature:
   1. Senior Project Manager and/or Project Manager (if applicable)
   2. Operations Manager
   3. Director of Contract Administration (and outside legal counsel if applicable)
   4. Director of Preconstruction
   5. Chief Financial Officer
4. Please review the [Contract Management Approval Cycle chart](https://robinsmorton.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/operations/PolicyAndProcedures/II.%20OPERATIONS/B.%20PROJECT%20START-UP/Contract%20Management%20Approval%20Cycle%20Chart%20-%20MM's%20Changes.xlsx?d=w76f9fe73990d4db1aef87dbd1bbff46c\&csf=1\&web=1\&e=cohMg1).
5. Signed Contract should be returned to the Director of Contract Administration.
