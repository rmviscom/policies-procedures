# Company Store

### Concepts & Associates is the authorized distributor for company store merchandise.&#x20;

The online store can be found via the Launchpad in HUB or by visiting [http://rmcs.conceptsusa.com](http://rmcs.conceptsusa.com).&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/companystore.png" alt="" width="188"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The online store provides team members with Robins & Morton logoed apparel, drinkware, office products and much more.&#x20;

The company store stock is continuously monitored and updated based on season and company needs. **All promotional items featuring the Robins & Morton brand come from Concepts & Associates.**&#x20;

If there is an item that is missing or a suggestion for improvement, please send to [Amanda Bradley Witteck](mailto:abradley@robinsmorton.com).&#x20;

## Outside vendor approval

If for some reason Concepts cannot fulfill an order, an approved vendor can be used. All promotional item use of the Robins & Morton brand must be sent to [Amanda Bradley Witteck](mailto:abradley@robinsmorton.com) and [Jacquie Stamper](mailto:jharty@robinsmorton.com) for approval of placement, color, and embroidery.&#x20;
